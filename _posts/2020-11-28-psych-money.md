This is about [The Psychology of Money](https://www.amazon.in/gp/product/9390166268/) by [Morgan Housel](https://www.collaborativefund.com/blog/authors/morgan/), a Columnist. This book became a sensation in the [#fintwit](https://twitter.com/hashtag/fintwit?lang=en). Many tweeters recommended the book and I preordered it on Amazon. The book is crisp - 240 pages grouped into 20 chapters, each having a story to explain actions behind money decisions.
