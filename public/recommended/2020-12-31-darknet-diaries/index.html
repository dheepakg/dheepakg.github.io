Darknet Diaries

Dheepak Govindaraj
2020-12-31
Podcast

This podcast is hosted by Jack Rhysider. Each of the episodes deals with events in cyberspace.

My favourite episode is the one that covered the Bangladesh Bank Heist.

There is no coding involved, Jack explains the strange/new words in layman terms. You should give a try if you’re curious about what’s happening in cyber space.

PS: Darknet Diaries has no relation with the darknet, the one accessed through Tor.
