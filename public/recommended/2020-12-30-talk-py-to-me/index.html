Talk Python To Me

Dheepak Govindaraj
2020-12-30
Podcast

This is the 1st ever podcast that I started with. I’m listening to the show regularly since 2017, yet I couldn’t recall how I got introduced to this show.

According to the talkpython.fm,

Talk Python to Me is a weekly podcast hosted by Michael Kennedy. The show covers a wide array of Python topics as well as many related topics (e.g. MongoDB, AngularJS, DevOps).

Michael Kennedy hosts this show and he conducts trainings as well. This is the most popular podcast on Python programming language.

Some of the hosts includes,

  • Al Sweigart of Automate the boring stuff with Python
  • Dan Bader of Real Python
  • Matthiass​ ​Bussonnier of Project Jupyter
  • Jake VanderPlas of Altair

My favourite episode is the one with Real Python’s Dan Bader on top 10 articles of 2019.

Python programmers of all levels should listen to this weekly podcast to gain insights from industry experts.
